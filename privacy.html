Privacy Policy

Data Collection

This add-on does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Google Meet Integration

The add-on runs within Google Meet's iframe sandbox. It uses Google Meet's Co-Doing API to synchronize session state between meeting participants. This state data is transient and managed entirely by Google's infrastructure.

No Analytics

No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind is used.

No Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties. The add-on communicates only with Google Meet's APIs.
